Colors Theme
---------------------------------------------------------------
Primary Colors:

    Dark Blue or Navy (#1B263B):the header, main buttons, and focus elements.

Secondary Colors:

    Burgundy or Deep Red (#800020): secondary buttons or hover states on primary buttons. 
    Slate Gray (#708090): Apply this to text elements, form borders, or secondary text. 

Accent Colors:

    Gold or Mustard (#DAA520): This should be used sparingly for highlight elements or icons.
    Light Gray (#D3D3D3): Ideal for form backgrounds or dividers.
    White (#FFFFFF): Use for main backgrounds, input fields.

Alert Colors:

    Bright Red (#FF4500): error messages, form validation errors, and critical alerts.
